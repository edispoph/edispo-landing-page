About Edispo

Edispo is the Philippine's first online e-waste exchange that allows households and MSMEs to properly dispose and sell, have different options, negotiate for a better price, enjoy hassle-free pick-up, and help build a clean and green environment.

  • A marketplace for consumer-to-consumer (C2C), consumer-to-business (C2B), and business-to-business (B2B) that encourages & promotes effective e-waste management by way of innovation and inclusion.
  • An enabler for junkshops, buyers, suppliers, brokers/agents, middlemen, consolidators, and traders to have access to a wider range of listings (in pieces or bulk/lot), sort and filter listings, negotiate on the price, advertise, and much more.
  • Serves as a Donation Portal where e-waste are coordinated directly with partner NGOs, private organizations and LGUs for proper disposal.
  • Offers rewards/e-points for sellers and donors through our cash-back-rewards partner
  • Introduces a data-sharing concept and Blockchain technology for efficiency, integrity, security, and analytics.

In the Philippines about 2 to 5 kilos of e-waste were produced per inhabitant, according to the report.
