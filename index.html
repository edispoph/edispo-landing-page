What is Edispo?

EDISPO is an online peer-to-peer marketplace focused on addressing the growing amount of e-waste in the Philippines.

We enable individuals, businesses, and Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to dispose of, buy, sell, trade, and negotiate all kinds of electronic and electrical goods. Whether that be working, non-working, industrial volume, individual, and all other items that are classified as e-waste.
