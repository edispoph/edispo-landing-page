What is Edispo?

EDISPO is an online peer-to-peer marketplace focused on addressing the growing amount of e-waste in the Philippines.

We enable individuals, businesses, and Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to dispose of, buy, sell, trade, and negotiate all kinds of electronic and electrical goods. Whether that be working, non-working, industrial volume, individual, and all other items that are classified as e-waste.

Whether you're a business worth millions of pesos, or an ordinary person living an ordinary life...

E-Waste has become a real problem

A 2016 study states that on average, one Filipino produces 2-5 kilograms of e-waste per year. While that may not sound like much, consider that there are approximately 67 million adult Filipinos today. That brings us to 335 MILLION KILOGRAMS of e-waste per year thrown out by Filipinos.


Because of the quick pace of technology, most people no longer have their gadgets and appliances fixed, they prefer to throw it away and purchase new items instead.


This is why we're here. To give used gadgets and appliances a new lease on life, or at least find responsible ways of recycling and upcycling these items.
